Cuisine

There are too many cuisines in Chinese cuisine and the Nanjing cuisine is also the same. I would introduce the top famous Nanjing dishes by the serving order of meal.

Tips: In fact, no one would have all of this dishes in this order, just a way to sort dishes by their category.


Salted Duck

Salted is one of the Nanjing specialty.
The duck meat is white and tender, fat but not greasy, and delicious.

Soup dumpling

Nanjing soup dumpling is a bit different from other places. Nanjing soup dumpling's soup is chicken soup.
That made Nanjing soup dumping more delicious than other soup dumping.

hotchpotch noodle

A huge bowl of noodle dish for four people! This noodle could be only found in Nanjing. It would be a good place for people who wanna have a eating challenge

Duck blood vermicelli soup

The Duck blood fan soup is the iconic food of Nanjing.
This dish was made of vermicelli, duck soup, processed duck blood and processed duck haslet.
It's sound a little bit horrible but really a delicious dish. Although there are some duck haslet in this dish, this dish is not fishy at all.
