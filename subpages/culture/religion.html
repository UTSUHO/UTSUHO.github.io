Buddhism

Buddhism was the strongest religion power in Nanjing due to historical and gephorical reasons. There are many buddhistic cultural attractions in Nanjing. Buddhist culture flows into Nanjing, beginning with the end of the Eastern Han Dynasty (189-220). Although Nanjing was built only 2480 years ago, Buddhism has come to Nanjing for 1780 years.


Christianity

Christianity is also an old face to Nanjing. The Christian believers currently operating in Nanjing City are mainly divided into two major denominations: Roman Catholicism and Christian Protestantism. The first missionary to establish a long-term mission in Nanjing was the Mainland Missionary Mr.G.Duncan. In 1867, shortly after the Taiping Heavenly Kingdom War was quelled, he entered Nanjing alone and lived in a temple rental house in the Gulou district. His pioneering work was rather difficult. Until 1882, the Mainland will transfer Nanjing work to other new arrivals.

Islam

Chinese Islam has a long history. In the 7th century AD, there were Arab Muslims living in China. In this way, Islam has appeared in China for more than 1,300 years. The spread of Islam in China began in the Yuan Dynasty and began to localize in the Ming Dynasty. In 700 years, Islam has become one of China's traditional religions. Chinese Islamic culture is also an important part of Chinese culture. Islam is a new face to Nanjing. Only a few of Nanjing citizens believe in Islam.

Local religion

There should be no sign for the local religion, this one was made by myself to apply the C.R.A.P rule.

Nanjing local region is mixed. Due to the historical reason, Taoism has not been respected by the ruling class of Nanjing in ancient times. Taoism and native religion was mixed into the current form of local religion. It includes land god religion, taoism religion, guan yu religion, and confucius religion
