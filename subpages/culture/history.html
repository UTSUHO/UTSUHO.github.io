Nanjing has build the city for mor than 2500 years. In the history of China, there were twelve dynasties who had settled here. Nanjing was called Yingtian Prefecture and the Ming dynasty changed that name to nanjing after the moving of the capital at yongle period. In the early Qing Dynasty, Yingtian Prefecture was converted to Jiangning Prefecture. In the early years of the Republic of China, Jiangning Prefecture was changed to Nanjing Prefecture, and Nanjing officially became the administrative district name. In 1927, it was named Nanjing Special City and was the capital of the Republic of China. After the founding of the People's Republic of China, the administrative divisions of Nanjing City had undergone many adjustments. As Lishui County and Gaochun County were incorporated into Nanjing City in 1983, the Nanjing jurisdiction reached the current scale.
