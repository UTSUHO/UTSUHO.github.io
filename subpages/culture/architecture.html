In 1927, the National Government of the Republic of China was established in Nanjing, then many Republican buildings were left. Along the Yangtze River Road, Zhongshan East Road, and Zhongshan North Road, the administrative office buildings of the various departments of the Nationalist Government are densely distributed. Most of them are imitation classical styles or Chinese-Western styles, and some are modernist styles. Now we call this building style as Republic of China Architecture Style.
