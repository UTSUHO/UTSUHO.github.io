Confucius Temple

This is a Chinese temple-style building dedicated to commemorating Confucius and offering sacrifices to Confucius for later generations. Since Hanwu emperor dismissed one hundred families, this temple became the temple which promote Confucianism. Nanjing confucius temple is located at Gongyuan Street, North Bank of Qinhuai River, Nanjing City. Nanjing citizen always celebrate Lantern Festival here. At that time, here would be some lantern shows and cruise ship travel to celebrate Lantern Festival.


Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum

Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum is the tomb of Sun Yat-sen who is the founder of the Republic of China and the forerunner of the Chinese democratic revolution. It is located in the south of Zijin Mountain in the eastern outskirts of Nanjing, Jiangsu Province. It is adjacent to Linggu Temple in the east and Ming Xiaoling Mausoleum in the west. After Sun Yat-sen passed away in Beijing in March 1925, the Chinese Kuomintang complied with his wishes and built a tomb for him in Nanjing. Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum was built in January 1926. It was built in the spring of 1929 and the main building was built. On June 1 of that year, Sun Yat-sen was buried and all the tombs were completed in 1931.

Zifeng Tower

Greenland Center Zifeng Tower or Greenland Square-Zifeng Tower is a skyscraper located in Gulou Square, Gulou District, Nanjing City, capital city of Jiangsu Province, China. This skyscraper is the 12th highest building of the world.
