Zijin Mountain

Zijin Mountain, also known as Jiangshan, Zhongshan, Shenlie Mountain, is located in the east of Nanjing, Jiangsu, China, about 7 kilometers from east to west, about 3 kilometers from north to south, and covers an area of more than 20 square kilometers. The peak height of the main peak is 448.2 meters.


Lao shan

Laoshan is a low mountain with beautiful scenery which located in Pukou District, Nanjing, which is 20 kilometers long, 3-5 kilometers wide and has an area of about 700,000 square kilometers. Developed and operated by Laoshan Forest Farm, Nanjing Laoshan National Forest Park was established in 1991.


Xuanwu Lake

Xuanwu Lake is located in Nanjing, Jiangsu Province. Before the Sixth Dynasty, it was known as Sangpo. It was once the largest imperial garden lake in China and the only remaining royal garden in the south of the Yangtze River. With the West Lake in Hangzhou, Jiaxing South Lake and called "the three famous lakes in the south of the Yangtze River." Xuanwu Lake Park is the largest city park in Nanjing.
